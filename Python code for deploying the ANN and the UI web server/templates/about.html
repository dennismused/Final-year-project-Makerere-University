Is your Queen Bee Present?

Welcome to D&A BEEHIVE MONITORING, the company that provides smart beehive monitoring systems for real-time monitoring. Our beehive monitoring systems usethe concept of machine learning to analyse and classify the sound from your beehive in order to keep you updated in real-time about the state of your beehive. Whether you are close to your hive or not, we shall keep you informed about about queen-state of your hive.


Our mission is to help preserve and support the honey bees, which are vital for the natural ecosystem and human life. We believe that by using our systems, beekeepers can improve their productivity and profitability, while also contributing to the conservation of these amazing creatures. In case you didn't know, the queen bee lays up to around 2,000 eggs a day. Lossing her means no more replacement for the dying bees. You need to know, in realitime, when she dies so that you can make timely intervention, and maybe replace her in time. You won't only be on your journey towards maximizing production in your hive, but also making sure the number of pollinators of your crops doesnt go down; in case you grow crops as well.
